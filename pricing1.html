What are the benefits of filtering traffic?

Bot is the biggest threat to online advertising as it damage trust with your partners and steal your ad spend.

The benefits are many:

  • improve lead quality and bring trust to your partners.
  • protect your stats with only relevant traffic.
  • protect your marketing funnel from your competition
How are you different from other filtering solutions?

First, our approach is completely different. Most competitors require a code installation on your server. We are the first cloud-based solution that use reverse proxy for integration. That means :

  • you can use on top of your current tools ( tracker ) without changing anything. Easiest integration ever.
  • you don't need to install any code on your server.
  • it's 10x faster as we filter traffic at the DNS level ( 30ms latency vs 500+ms for competitors )
  • Client side are either absent or are a mess with competitors ( high clicks loss )

So in short, our advantages are easier integration, faster delivery, full client side protection and low bleed-rate.

How does it works?

To explain simply, there are two types of check we can do to detect bots:

  • what we call server-side rely on variable such as IP, location, language, SSL handshake, user agent. These are quite easy to spoof for advanced bot and you cannot rely only on these.
  • what we call client-side are checks perform on the user browser. It's harder to spoof as there is a lot of variable we can check about the user device to determine if it's real or not.

in our opinions, the current bot filtering solution on the market are focus on server-side checks, while client-side checks are mostly ignored as the integration is harder.

So to recap: We support the same server-side checks features ( geo targeting, device filtering, bot detection ) plus advanced client side detection ( headless browser ). Moreover the integration is seamless and can plug easily with your current funnel solution. There is absolutely no code to setup. Instead you simply CNAME your domain to our us. What else? the UI is incredibly easy to use for managing campaign at massive scale.
